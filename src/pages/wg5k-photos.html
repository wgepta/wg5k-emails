2018 Willow Glen 5K

Saturday, May 12, 2018

Run for education!

Thank you!

On behalf of the Willow Glen 5K team, we'd like to thank you again for joining us this year. Our mission to raise funds for local public schools could not have been done without you!

The Willow Glen 5K is organized and managed by local school parents and is staffed by hundreds of community members, school parents and student volunteers - a near-year round effort.

Sponsors

This year's Willow Glen 5K was made possible again by our Presenting Sponsor, San Jose Swim and Racquet Club, and the generous contributions and donations from our many Willow Glen businesses (see sidebar on right).

Join us in thanking them by supporting them the next time you're in Willow Glen!

Race day photos

Check out some of the photo highlights for this year.

In addition to photos from the start and finish, race course and festival, photos of most racers can be viewed by searching using your race bib number. Forgot your number? Check out the race results page to look it up.

Download photos, email them to friends and family, or post them on social media by selecting your photo and clicking the icons at the bottom-left corner.

Photos and other photo-related products can also be purchased clicking on the Order Print button.

Race results

Check out the race results! Type your name or bib number in the Search box to find your time and place. Or, click on the Overall tab and view all results.
