What is a virtual race?

Racing at a distance

Each racer is asked to run on their own or with their family. While we want to be precise with our recommendations, information is changing every day. For the most current information, please follow guidelines outlined by Santa Clara County.

Any day or every day, as long as it's in May!

Don't worry about meeting on Saturday at 9AM. Run whenever you want in May. We're adding resources on where to run if you need help finding a course.

Race bags will be mailed to your home

About the Willow Glen 5K

Willow Glen 5K's flat and USATF-certified race course starts in San Jose’s Willow Glen downtown and winds through neighborhood streets, with racers cheered on by local businesses and residents.

Racers, families and neighbors are then invited to a post-race Festival, awards ceremony and Kids' Fun Zone. Festival activities include sponsor booths, pancake breakfast, music, photo booth, chiropractic and more. Kids’ Fun Zone includes games, activities and slide. Both spread out over two areas of the Willow Glen Elementary school campus.

Proceeds from the race go to local elementary school PTAs to fund after-school programs such as: arts, music, science, sports, technology and many others.
