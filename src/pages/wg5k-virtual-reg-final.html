--- subject: Thank you for signing up for our virtual 5K - Willow Glen 5K ---

Thank you for making our virtual 5K a success!

There's no doubt this year has been unexpected in so many ways, but we're going to make it through ... together.

On behalf of the Willow Glen 5K team, we'd like to thank you again for joining us this year. Our mission to raise funds for local public schools could not have been done without you!

Support us on social media

Remember, if you post about the race, tag us with #wg5k2020

Enter your time

Just because we're not running together doesn't mean you can't keep track of your time. Time your run and enter it on our race website.

Use this link: https://results.raceroster.com/results/pemy5434cybpz623

Race bags are coming!

We know some of you are still waiting for your race bags! They're coming. We expect the last batch to be delivered this weekend so check your mailbox. Clearly, this year has been a bit different so we thank you for being patient with us.

Finding or sharing a course

We're all finding our own way in these uncertain times and that's the same for the Willow Glen 5K this year. But that doesn't mean you have to do it all by yourself.

If you know a 5K course that you like, share it with the others. You can't run together but you can still race.

If you don't know any courses, check out our race course pages to pick one you like. Check back often as we'll be adding new courses frequently.

Virtual Race Bags

Some sponsors have created virtual race bags with coupons or additional information. Check them out!

Willow Glen 5K is made possible by contributions from Willow Glen businesses. COVID-19 is affecting everyone, including our sponsors.
